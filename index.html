Organizations and interests

keep track of events from your organizations and interests.

Are you tired of checking your emails and finding events from different newsletters? Do you want a easier way to view events you might be interested in?

Try Happening for an easier solution. With Happening, all you need to do is select all your organizations and set up your interest tags, and a single click will list all the events for your just like magic.

Search and filter

Explore events specially for you.

Want to find something during your free time? Happening does it all for you. Its extensive search and filter functions allows you to find specific events by date, interests and organizations.

Whether you want to find something random on a relaxing afternoon or you want to enjoy some live music at a specific evening, Happening will help you find your need.

Manage your events

Sort and manage your events has never been so easy before.

Once you find a campus event you would like to attend, just click on the save or RSVP button to mark it to your saved events page. No need to had type everything into your calendar or overload your memory, Happening can help you to manage all your events.

Its sophisticated saved events page gives a clear view of all your future events and allows you to easily manage them with a few tab.
